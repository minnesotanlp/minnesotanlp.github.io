Welcome to Minnesota NLP

A research group led by Dongyeop Kang and Alexander Spangher (joining Fall 2026) in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Minnesota – Twin Cities. We are an inter-disciplinary team drawing from linguistics, social sciences, and cognitive sciences to develop state-of-the-art AI and support expert workflows in law, science, education, journalism, and beyond.
